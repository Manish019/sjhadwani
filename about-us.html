S Jadhwani Group

The Jadhwani Group was established in 1985 and is founded and headed by Mr. Nihal Jadhwani.

M/s. Jadhwani Builders & Developers formed in 1985 now forayed into S Jadhwani Developers is managed by Mr. Sandeep Jadhwani under the leadership of his father.

The Jadhwani Group was established in 1985 and is founded & headed by Mr. Nihal Jadhwani has been in the Real Estate business for over 30 years. The group has made various projects under the company M/s. Jadhwani Builders & Developers formally. The Group have forayed into a new entity S Jadhwani Developers with the joining of Mr. Sandeep Jadhwani. A young new generation entrepreneur with management skills and professional expertise. There is an ideal mix of old generation experience & new generation dynamism in the decision making process. The Organisation has a strong working knowledge in the Construction Development, investment analysis & various financial structures.

Today the group is managed by an able team of professionals from various fields and has the ability to solve problems as they arise. The Group has an in-house legal team and architects. With a vision to build better lives, S Jadhwani Developers exceeds the expectations of customers through innovative, world class solutions, thereby creating value at the most opportunate moment. In keeping with the exponential growth plans, the Group will soon launch various other exclusive projects.
